Scratch Online/Archive

The Archive for Scratch Projects

Here, I will be choosing only the best scratch projects and put them onto this website. If you want a game updated or you want a new game please put it on the issues page: https://github.com/AwesomeKalin/scratch-online/issues You may also put bug reports for my own projects! But DO NOT submit bug reports for other projects as it's not my fault.
To launch downloaded games, extract the zip, tar.gz of 7z file (based on which one you downloaded) and double click the index.html.
THIS REQUIRES A HTML5 COMPATIBLE BROWSER!!!
